6575 days on Earth

2020-09-17
Are you still alive? Yes, you!
Long? that's just the beginning, nothing. the journey is just about to begin, brace yourself! the universe isn't that kidding, realize!

How good does it feel when the birthday comes, birthday cake? friend? or even a party? it will all come automatically like a machine, aka a celebration and its partner, especially if it’s not a surprise.
BUT IT WAS, very long … so long.

Now? crazy! I just don’t want to do that anymore, it’s nothing, it’s just that when that day comes again and again I feel that there is an increasing burden and I am required to be mature, yes that’s a consequence that must be accepted. Is not it?? or maybe just me? Ah I don’t think so, but I don’t know.

Every time that day comes I always think 'what have I done? (For myself and others)' often confused to answer the question, not to mention other questions (which came from my own head of course).

But it’s still the very foundation of what comes out of my mind, I will feel like a person having a panic attack when the question of how my future is, not only briefly but I sometimes think about it until I get stressed.
it sounds stupid, but that’s how stupid I am.
Think things are still far ahead, you idiot!

now to the point, load.
the number of days is increasing, the burden will be heavier and closer, yes how could the pigeon mother find food for her children, and her child will find food for her, not to mention if the child is not alone, aka having a smaller sibling, it must be a challenges for children who have smaller siblings. thoughts of ‘can I find food for my parents and younger siblings?’ it always popped into the child’s head.
Wait, what ?? The pigeon is not like that
yes, I also know, but that is just a picture, with what I am experiencing at this time. the child’s mind is the same as mine, damn it!

well, that’s a little what’s on my mind.
